Cherry Brownie Recipe

Chocolate Cherry Brownies

This dish is comprised of some delicious cherries and chocolate chips on top of the classic brownie.

This recipe is easy and super simple to follow and does not require too many ingredients. Most of your time will include getting the batter ready for preparations. Let's get started!

Ingredients:

  • Betty Crocker Original Supreme brownie mix
  • water
  • vegetable oil
  • 4 eggs
  • heavy whipping cream- one cup
  • mini marshmallows- one cup
  • chopped maraschino cherries- 1/3 cup and well drained
  • chocolate chip topping

Directions:

  1. Mix, bake, & make brownies first. Follow directions on the Betty Crocker box for making them
  2. While making brownies, in medium bowl, beat whipping cream with electric mixer eon high speed until stiff
  3. Gently stir in marshmallows and cherries
  4. Spread evenly over brownies once cooled
  5. Add chocolate chips on top. Tip: put chips in a sealed bag and heat in microwave from 20-30 sec. Once melted, cut corner off with scissors. Then drizzle the chocolate overthe brownies
  6. Optional: Swirl chocolate through whipped cream mixture with spoon or knife
  7. Cover and refrigerate about one hour or until chilled
  8. Serve and Enjoy (=^ w ^=)
